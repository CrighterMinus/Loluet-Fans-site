Who is LOLUET?

LOLUET, name unknown, is a singer from Japan. Known as an Utaite, she started off recording herself singing covers of songs and sharing them on niconico. At this point in her career, she was known under a different name: Reciel. From 2018 onward, along with posting on niconico, Reciel also released covers onto sites like youtube, soundcloud, and even twitter.

After a few years of this, a name change occurred. On the 18th day of May, 2021, LOLUET was born. A new Youtube account, two new Twitter accounts, a new niconico account, and more came into existence. Since then, LOLUET has been releasing covers sporadically, with the occasional collaboration with large artists such as biz, TeddyLoid, Giga, and more. There have been breaks and nuances in her history between then and now, but that can all be examined carefully to your hearts content in the Complete LOLUET Timeline.
